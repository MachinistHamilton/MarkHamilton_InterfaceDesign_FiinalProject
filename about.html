Our Story

WarBrush Studios was founded by passionate hobbyists with a vision. We believe every miniature should look as legendary as the story it tells. What began as a personal obsession with detail grew into a full-time studio. Our mission is to elevate your collection with professional, hand-painted art. Whether you're a gamer, collector, or enthusiast — we bring your vision to life. Trusted worldwide, we turn plastic and resin into battlefield icons.

Our Method

Each commission is a conversation. We don’t just paint — we translate your vision into color, texture, and presence on the battlefield. With a strong foundation in color theory, miniature realism, and material experimentation, our process brings cinematic depth to every piece.
